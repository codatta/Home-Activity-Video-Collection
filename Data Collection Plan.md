# 📹 Codatta Home Scene Video Data Collection

## 1. Campaign Overview

**Objective**: Collect high-quality first-person POV video data of daily home activities for AI model training.

**Scenes** (choose one or more):
1. 🍳 **Kitchen Cooking** - Cutting, stir-frying, seasoning, plating
2. 🧹 **House Tidying** - Organizing clothes, storing items, desk cleanup

---

## 2. Equipment & Recording Specifications

### 📱 Device Requirements

| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Resolution | 1080P+ (1920×1080) |
| Color | Color, normal exposure, no over/under exposure |
| Duration | Each video ≥ 10 minutes |

### 🎥 Mounting Position

**Recommended** (choose one):
1. **Chest Mount** - Use chest strap/harness, camera at chest center
2. **Head Mount** - Use headband/cap, camera at forehead center

**Camera Direction**:
- Lens facing forward, tilted down slightly (15-30°)
- Ensure both hands and objects stay in frame
- Avoid excessive shaking

---

## 3. Quality Acceptance Criteria

### ✅ Valid Videos Must Meet

| Check | Standard |
|-------|----------|
| Resolution | ≥ 1920×1080 |
| Duration | ≥ 10 minutes |
| Orientation | Horizontally |
| Stability | No excessive shaking/blur |
| Exposure | Good lighting, no dark/overexposed |
| Hands Visible | Both hands in frame throughout |
| Object Visible | Operated object clearly visible |
| Continuity | Continuous recording, no frequent stops |

### ❌ Invalid Cases

- Resolution below 1080P
- Duration under 10 minutes
- Hands frequently out of frame
- Too dark/bright to identify content
- Corrupted/unplayable video

## 5. FAQ

**Q1: Can I use a smartphone?**
A: Yes! Ensure phone is mounted on chest or head, use rear camera, set resolution to 1080P or higher.

**Q2: Do I need to record continuously for 10 minutes?**
A: Yes, each video must be at least 10 minutes continuous recording without frequent pauses.

**Q3: Can I edit the video?**
A: Not recommended. Submit raw footage without editing or post-processing.

**Q4: What if lighting is poor?**
A: Choose daytime or turn on indoor lights to ensure clear footage.

**Q5: Can multiple people record together?**
A: Yes, but each video should have one main operator (hands in frame).
