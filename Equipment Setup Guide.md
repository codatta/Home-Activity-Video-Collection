## Option 1: Chest Mount (Recommended ⭐)

### 🛒 Buy Ready-Made

**Search Keywords**:
- "chest mount phone holder"
- "first person view filming gadget"
- "body camera mount"
- “chest strap + phone clip”

---

### 🔧 DIY Alternative

**Materials Needed**:
- Backpack chest strap (existing)
- Car phone holder or desk stand
- Zip ties / strong tape

**Steps**:
1. Attach phone holder to center of chest strap
2. Secure with zip ties
3. Adjust angle to point forward-downward

---

## Option 2: Head Mount

### 🛒 Buy Ready-Made

**Search Keywords**:
- "head strap phone mount"
- "POV camera headband"
- "First-person view head mount"


**Tip**: Choose adjustable size for comfort

---

### 🔧 DIY Version

**Materials**:
- Sports headband / elastic band
- Phone clamp
- Velcro / zip ties

**Steps**:
1. Fix phone mount at center of headband
2. Balance weight to prevent tilting
3. Test stability (shake head gently)

---

## Option 3: Cap Modification (Creative)

**Materials**:
- Baseball cap / cap with brim
- Small phone clip
- Strong glue / sewing kit

**Steps**:
1. Mount phone clip above brim center
2. Ensure firm attachment
3. Adjust shooting angle

## 📐 Camera Angle Guide

### Correct Angle

    Camera
      ↓ 15-30°
    👐👐
    Object

**First-person perspective is required**
 
### Checklist
- [ ] Both hands visible in frame
- [ ] Object being operated is clear
- [ ] No excessive shaking
- [ ] Comfortable for 10+ minutes
---
## 💡 Practical Tips
1. **Test First**: Record 1 minute to check angle
2. **Good Lighting**: Daytime or turn on lights
3. **Secure Mount**: Avoid loosening during recording
4. **Power Bank**: Prepare for long recordings
5. **Storage**: Ensure enough phone space
